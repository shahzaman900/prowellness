Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) platforms collect and transmit sensitive patient data, including vital signs, medical history, and treatment information. A data breach in this system could have devastating consequences, exposing patients to identity theft, fraudulent medical claims, and even emotional distress.

At ProactiveWellness, we understand the importance of continuous health monitoring while safeguarding your patients’ privacy. That’s why we prioritize robust cybersecurity measures in our

HIPAA-Compliant

RPM platform

HIPAA Compliance:

We adhere to strict HIPAA regulations, ensuring all patient data is protected with industry-standard protocols.

SOC 2 Compliance:

We are SOC 2 compliant for all the controls that are applicable to HIPAA regulations.

Secure Data Transmission:

We ensure communication channels have end-to-end encryptions to safeguard data transfer between patients’ devices and our platform.

Multi-Layered Security:

We implement a comprehensive security infrastructure that allows specific access controls according to the necessary standards

Security Audits:

We maintain a

proactive

approach by conducting regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Choosing the Right Partner for Secure RPM:

When selecting an RPM platform, cybersecurity should be a top priority. Here are some questions to consider:

Does the vendor prioritize HIPAA compliance?

What security measures are in place to protect patient data?

How does the platform handle data encryption and transmission?

Does the vendor conduct regular security audits?
